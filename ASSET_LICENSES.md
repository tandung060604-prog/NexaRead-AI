## Recommended Sources for Production Assets

- **Freesound.org** — CC0 and Creative Commons licensed audio
- **Pixabay** — Royalty-free music and ambient sounds
- **Unsplash** — Free high-resolution photography
- **Google Fonts** — Open-source web fonts
- **Self-generated** — Web Audio API procedural sounds

## Usage Guidelines

1. All audio must be either CC0, royalty-free, or self-created.
2. No copyrighted characters or brand imagery.
3. No YouTube-sourced video without explicit license.
4. No images without clear usage rights.
5. Document every asset replacement in this file.
